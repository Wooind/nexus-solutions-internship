# Nexus Solutions — Company Overview

**Industry:** IT Services and Consulting
**Location:** Cebu, Philippines
**Size:** Mid-sized (75–200 employees)

## What We Do

Nexus Solutions provides IT consulting, custom software development, and systems analysis services to clients across the Visayas region. Our clients include retail businesses, healthcare providers, and local government units.

## Departments

- IT Operations
- Software Development
- Business Analysis
- Sales and Client Relations
- Human Resources

## Our Internship Program

Nexus Solutions runs a structured internship program for BSIS and BSIT students. Interns are embedded in real project teams and treated as junior professionals from day one.
